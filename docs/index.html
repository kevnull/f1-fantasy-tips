Chip strategy
WildcardWait
If you need 3+ transfers to pivot to the Antonelli x2 + Mercedes + Ferrari constructor meta, the wildcard is justified here — especially after the 5-week break with upgrades reshuffling the field. Rain specifically notes being willing to use wildcard post-Limitless if taking a -20 hit. However, some suggest holding back until sprint qualifying reveals the new pecking order.
LimitlessSave
Most sources already used or planned Limitless for Japan. If you still have it, consensus is Canada or Monaco may be better targets — save it for a non-sprint weekend where the field is more stable post-upgrades. Rain used his in Japan to great effect (triple Mercedes + triple Ferrari + Piastri).
3x BoostWait
If sticking with a 2A-driver build for one more race, a 3x on one of the Mercedes drivers (Antonelli or Russell) plus a Ferrari/McLaren driver could be strong on a sprint weekend with three qualifying sessions generating extra points.
AutopilotWait
Viable if data from sprint qualifying is inconclusive and you have multiple strong A-tier drivers (e.g., Leclerc + Antonelli or Leclerc + Piastri). The system can optimize across sprint + race.
Final FixSave
Situational — only use if a key asset DNFs in sprint or qualifying. Otherwise save for a more critical moment.

Race arc
Miami is the critical inflection point of the early 2026 season. The 5-week break means every team brings upgrades, and sprint qualifying on Friday will be the first real data revealing the new competitive order. If Mercedes maintain dominance, the Antonelli x2 + Mercedes + Ferrari constructor meta solidifies and budget-builders should aggressively pursue that template. If McLaren's upgrades prove transformative, a more expensive McLaren+Mercedes build may emerge, potentially delaying the two-premium-constructor pivot. After Miami, there's another 3-week break before Canada (another sprint weekend), making it a natural Limitless or Wildcard target for those who haven't used them. Monaco follows shortly after as a unique low-overtake circuit. The key budget-building window is Miami through Canada — Lawson, Hulkenberg, and Ocon are the assets to lock in now for maximum price growth across this stretch.
